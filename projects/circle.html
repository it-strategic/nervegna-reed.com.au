projects » circle template house (2001)

A small house and artists studio designed for two artists. Extending the notion of the Working drawing-as-house façade (The Working Drawing House, St Kilda), the form of the architects ‘circle template’ (used for the manual drawing of circles) has been used, like a giant readymade, as a sunshade / brise soleil.
