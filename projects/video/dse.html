projects » video » the derrida signature event

The 'Derrida Signature Even' was a video piece made with Jacques Derrida for the Melbourne festival in 2000. The piece was filmed when Derrida was in Melbourne a few years before and then edited for the large multi-screen installation at the Arts Centre on St Kilda Road.
