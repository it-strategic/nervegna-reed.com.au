Mei Long Lake, Nanjing - Master Plan
2015
Master Plan – Nervegna Reed, USDG
Landscape Architects – Urban Initiatives

The Mei Long Lake master plan was part of a limited competition for a site in the suburbs of Nanjing. The focus of the project was to revitalise the lake and the area surrounding the lake. Nervegna Reed helped design the master plan with USDG and landscape architects Urban Initiatives, while also focusing on the architecture and infra structure of the site, including the new pedestrian bridge, lake buildings and sculptures and the conceptualisation of the island Tea House and restaurants. The project has been put on hold for the present.
